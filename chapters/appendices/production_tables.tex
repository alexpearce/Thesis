\chapter{Charm production yield, efficiency, and systematic tables}
\label{chap:app:production_tables}

Prompt signal yields measured in charm hadron \pTy\ bins are given in 
\cref{tab:prod:fitting:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:fitting:DpToKpipi,tab:prod:fitting:DsToKKpi,tab:prod:fitting:DstToD0pi_D0ToKpi} 
for \PDzero, \PDplus, \PDsplus, and \PDstarp mesons, measured using the fit 
procedure described in \cref{chap:prod:fitting}.

The following efficiencies are given for \PDzero and \PDplus: acceptance 
efficiencies in \cref{tab:prod:effs:acc:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:acc:dptokpipi}; 
reconstruction efficiencies in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:reco:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:reco:dptokpipi}; tracking 
efficiency correction factors in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:tracking:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:tracking:dptokpipi}; 
selection efficiencies in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:sel:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:sel:dptokpipi}; \acl{PID} 
efficiencies in \cref{tab:prod:effs:pid:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:pid:dptokpipi}; 
and signal window efficiencies in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:sigwin:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:sigwin:dptokpipi}.
The computation of the efficiencies is described in \cref{chap:prod:effs}.

The following systematic uncertainties for \PDzero and \PDplus are presented:
due to the size of the finite \ac{MC} samples in 
\cref{tab:prod:syst:mc:stat:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:syst:mc:stat:DpToKpipi}; due to 
the finite size of the \ac{PID} calibration samples in 
\cref{tab:prod:syst:pid:stat:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:syst:pid:stat:DpToKpipi}; due to 
the choice of binning shecme in the \ac{PID} calibration procedure in 
\cref{tab:prod:syst:pid:binning:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:syst:pid:binning:DpToKpipi}; 
due to the tracking efficiency correction methods in 
\cref{tab:prod:syst:tracking:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:syst:tracking:DpToKpipi}; due to 
the choice of fit model in 
\cref{tab:prod:syst:fitting:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:syst:fitting:DpToKpipi}; and due 
to the statistical uncertainty on the model parameters used to evaluate the 
signal window efficiency in 
\cref{tab:prod:syst:signal_window:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:syst:signal_window:DpToKpipi}.
The computation of the systematic uncertainties is described in 
\cref{chap:prod:syst}.
