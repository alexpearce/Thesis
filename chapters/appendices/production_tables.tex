\chapter{Charm production yield and efficiency tables}
\label{chap:app:production_tables}

Prompt signal yields measured in charm hadron \pTy\ bins are given in 
\cref{tab:prod:fitting:D0ToKpi,tab:prod:fitting:DpToKpipi,tab:prod:fitting:DsToKKpi,tab:prod:fitting:DstToD0pi_D0ToKpi} 
for \PDzero, \PDplus, \PDsplus, and \PDstarp mesons, measured using the fit 
procedure described in \cref{chap:prod:fitting}.
The following efficiencies are given for \PDzero and \PDplus: acceptance 
efficiencies in \cref{tab:prod:effs:acc:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:acc:dptokpipi}; 
reconstruction efficiencies in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:reco:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:reco:dptokpipi}; tracking 
efficiency correction factors in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:tracking:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:tracking:dptokpipi}; 
selection efficiencies in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:sel:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:sel:dptokpipi}; \acl{PID} 
efficiencies in \cref{tab:prod:effs:pid:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:pid:dptokpipi}; 
and signal window efficiencies in 
\cref{tab:prod:effs:sigwin:dztokpi,tab:prod:effs:sigwin:dptokpipi}.
The computation of the efficiencies is described in \cref{chap:prod:effs}.
